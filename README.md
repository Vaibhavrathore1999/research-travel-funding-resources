# Awesome ML/AI Conference Travel Grants

Short, community-maintained list of travel funding, fee waivers, and volunteering options for researchers attending ML/AI conferences (NeurIPS, CVPR, ICML, ICLR, etc.). PRs and updates welcome.

Why this repo
- Helps researchers (especially from low-resource regions) find active travel grants, fee waivers, and volunteer opportunities.

## Quick Contents
- Government Funding
- Institute-Level Support
- Industry Travel Grants
- Conference Aid & Fee Waivers
- Volunteer Programs
- Fellowships & Domain Grants
- How to Contribute
- Acknowledgements

## Notes
- Many industry travel grants are paused or seasonal — check official portals before applying.
- Institute support varies by department; contact your supervisor or Dean (R&D).

## How to contribute
1. Fork and open a PR adding or updating an entry.
2. Use this format:
	- Grant name
	- Link
	- Status (active/paused)
	- Covers (registration/travel/visa)
	- Notes (deadlines, eligibility)
